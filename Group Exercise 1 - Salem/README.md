Gas Station Calculator

How to Run 

Visual Studio:

Open Visual Studio

Create new Console App

Copy the code from GasStationCalculator.cs

Press F5

What It Does

Asks for gas station name
Asks for price per gallon
Asks how many gallons
Asks for tax percentage
Calculates subtotal, tax, and total
Shows a receipt




Sample Output
Enter the name of gas station: Gas 4 Less
Enter the price of gas: 4.99
How many gallons: 10
What is the tax percentage for this transaction: 8.25

******* Gas 4 Less *******
Gas price: $4.99
Number of gallons: 10
Tax rate: 8.25%
Subtotal: $49.90
Tax: $4.12
Total: $54.02
Calculations Used

Subtotal = price per gallon × gallons
Tax = subtotal × (tax percentage ÷ 100)
Total = subtotal + tax
